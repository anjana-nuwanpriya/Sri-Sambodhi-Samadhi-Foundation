Heladiriya: Building a Stronger and Resourceful Future for Sri Lanka

Empowering rural communities through sustainable development, education, and spiritual upliftment rooted in Buddha Dhamma

Purpose of the Project

It has been reported that most of our rural population lives below the level of minimum living standards due to lack of realizing opportunities and potential for development. It is also a fact that they possess good talents, resources and values.

In this context, Sri Sambodhi Samadhi Foundation has developed a Vision under the name "Heladiriya" to achieve twin objectives of rural economic independence and spiritual upliftment to create a stronger and resourceful future generation for the benefit of our country.

As the temple is the focal point of the village, all related activities with regard to the above will be centered in association with the temple thereby, strengthening its relationship further with the villagers. This will eventually enable us to conserve and protect Dhamma, cultural values and heritage.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To identify the available resources and talents of villagers with a view to develop and uplift the living standards of the Sri Lankan rural population within the framework of the Buddha Dhamma.

Mission

It has been focused on the unemployed rural youth who are unable to realize their talents, values, resources and the potential to create sustainable self-employment job opportunities.

The Challenge We Address

It has been observed that due to the unplanned education system and prevalent social environment, the majority of the talented rural youth after their secondary education, do not have opportunities for development other than those who are able to influence them politically.

Hence chances of them achieving their own success is seldom and thus this system has closed the opportunities which could have helped the society to build a better Sri Lanka.

Our Approach

In this context, it was felt that a dedicated and motivated team could assist and guide the rural youth to explore their talents. For this purpose, "Sri Sambodhi Samadhi Foundation" was formed to support and develop the less fortunate talented rural youth.

The Heladiriya Initiative

All activities undertaken by this Foundation will be named as "Heladiriya" to depict the strength and vigour of the indigenous society. This initiative represents our commitment to:

  • Empower unemployed rural youth to realize their full potential
  • Create sustainable self-employment opportunities
  • Foster a new generation of young entrepreneurs
  • Preserve and strengthen Dhamma, culture, and heritage
  • Build economic independence in rural communities
  • Provide spiritual guidance through Buddhist teachings

Our Programs

Through our Training Institute, Leadership Academy, and Community Development Centres, we provide comprehensive programs that include:

  • Skills Training: Vocational training in various trades to enable youth to become self-employed
  • Leadership Development: Cultivating leadership qualities and entrepreneurial mindset
  • Spiritual Guidance: Education in Buddha Dhamma for holistic personal development
  • Community Development: Projects that strengthen village infrastructure and social cohesion
  • Cultural Preservation: Programs to conserve and promote Sri Lankan heritage

Temple-Centered Development

As the temple is the focal point of the village, we center all our activities in association with temples. This approach:

  • Strengthens the relationship between temples and villagers
  • Leverages the temple's respected position in the community
  • Ensures cultural and spiritual continuity
  • Protects and preserves Buddha Dhamma
  • Creates a trusted environment for learning and growth

Join Our Mission

We invite volunteers, donors, and partners to join us in this transformative journey. Together, we can create a brighter future for Sri Lanka's rural youth and strengthen the fabric of our nation.
